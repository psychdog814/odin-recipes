Baked Potato

Ingredients

  • 1 medium potato
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoon of butter
  • 1 pinch of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup shredded chedder cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F/150 degrees C
  2. Scrub the potato and pierce the skin several times with a knife or fork
  3. Rub the skin of olive oil
  4. Rub the skin with salt
  5. Place potato inside oven for about 90 minutes, potato should be soft and golden brown
  6. Cut potato down the middle, add butter and pepper
  7. OPTIONAL: add chedder cheese ontop
